Who we are
Nelson locals building for Nelson locals

South Build was founded in 2009 by the Mitchell family with one simple goal — to build quality homes and commercial spaces that Nelson families can be proud of for decades to come.

Over 15 years we've completed more than 200 projects across Nelson, Richmond, Stoke and the wider top of the South.

Common questions
FAQ
Do you offer fixed price contracts?
Yes — we offer fixed price contracts on most projects. The price we quote is the price you pay.
How long does a new build take?
Most new builds take between 6–12 months depending on size and complexity.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes — all our builders are Licensed Building Practitioners (LBP) and we carry full public liability insurance.
Do you work outside Nelson?
We primarily work in Nelson, Richmond, Stoke and the wider top of the South.
How much does a renovation cost?
Renovation costs vary widely. A bathroom might start from $15,000 while a full kitchen can range from $20,000–$60,000+.
